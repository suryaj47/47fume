How long does 47FUME last?

47FUME is crafted using premium fragrance oils, providing an impressive longevity of up to 12 hours depending on skin type, climate, and application.

Is 47FUME suitable for daily wear?

Absolutely. Its balanced composition makes it ideal for office, casual outings, evening events, and special occasions.

Yes. 47FUME is designed with a sophisticated blend of fresh, floral, and woody notes that can be enjoyed by anyone.
